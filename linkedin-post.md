I built the Schema Builder that Salesforce should have made.
Open-source, zero dependencies, right in your Chrome browser.

After years of fighting with Salesforce's native Schema Builder — the one that takes 30 seconds to load, shows you 200 objects you don't care about, and hasn't changed since 2015 — I decided to build something better.

Meet Schelio.

A Chrome extension that turns any Salesforce org into a modern, interactive ERD in seconds.

What it does:

- Interactive SVG canvas — zoom, drag & drop, force-directed auto-layout
- 8-tab detail panel per object: fields, record types, picklist dependencies, layouts, profiles, permission sets, validation rules (with formulas), flows & triggers, schema health score
- Cross-object field search across your entire data model
- Dependent picklist visualization with real-time filtering
- Profile & Permission Set permissions (CRUD + Field-Level Security)
- Schema health score with Salesforce limit warnings

6 one-click exports:
- PNG / SVG for presentations
- Mermaid & PlantUML for GitHub, Notion, Confluence
- CSV field inventory for spreadsheets
- PDF Technical Spec — a complete data model document with cover page, table of contents, field dictionary, and relationship map

Tech: Vanilla JS, zero dependencies, Manifest V3.
Nothing leaves your browser. No server, no tracking, no account needed.

The repo is public. Contributions welcome.

Link in the comments.
